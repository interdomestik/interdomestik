# Interdomestik v1.0.0 Bounded Continuation Plan

## Default

After the initial `v1.0.0` pilot window, the default decision is `pause`.

No continuation should happen by inertia.

## Continuation Is Allowed Only If

- the active pilot authority line is complete
- the latest observability state is `clear` or `expected-noise`
- no Sev1 exists
- no privacy, tenant, or branch boundary breach exists
- evidence custody stayed clean without post-hoc repair
- a fresh executive review states the continuation objective explicitly

## Required Continuation Statement

Any continuation beyond the initial bounded pilot window must name:

- the narrow objective
- the stop date or end condition
- the operator owner
- why the current evidence is insufficient to close cleanly now
- why expansion is still `no`, if expansion is not being approved

## Continuation Focus

The first bounded continuation after `v1.0.0`, if any, should focus on:

- clean evidence custody
- improved `2 Operating-Day Progression`
- re-proved privacy/RBAC boundary behavior through `PD05B`

## What Continuation Must Not Mean

- not tenant expansion
- not multi-branch expansion
- not relaxed boundary controls
- not inference-based promotion from a successful pilot tag

## Exit Conditions

End bounded continuation when one of these becomes true:

- the objective is met and executive closeout is complete
- a stop criterion is hit
- evidence custody degrades and cannot be restored within one operating day
- the executive review declines further continuation

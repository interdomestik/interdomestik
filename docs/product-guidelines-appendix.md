## Formula Library

### Formula A: Cold Traffic

`[Outcome] + [Membership] + [Utility] + [IDA Card proof]`

Example:

- `You're not alone after an accident. Start membership and use IDA App for guidance, claim packs, tracking, and your IDA Card inside the app.`

### Formula B: Checkout

`[Activate membership] + [what membership includes] + [immediate proof]`

Example:

- `Activate your membership. You'll get access to IDA App and your IDA Card will be ready inside the app after payment.`

### Formula C: Success

`[Membership active] + [utility ready] + [proof visible] + [next step]`

Example:

- `Membership active. Your access to IDA App is ready. View your IDA Card or continue in the app.`
